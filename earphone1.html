About Our Product

Can I use Buds VS104 as a single earbud while listening to music?
Yes, you can. Place one earbud inside the case and use the other one.
Do Buds VS104 have a touch feature to change tracks?
Yes, you can change tracks by tapping the earbuds. Touch and hold the left earbud for 2 seconds to go to the previous track. Touch and hold the right earbud for 2 seconds to go to the next track.
How is the calling?
Buds VS104 delivers a good call quality experience.
During calls is the audio available in both ears?
Yes, Buds VS104 supports stereo mode during calls and music as well.
Does it announce the caller name or number on incoming calls?
No, it doesn’t.
